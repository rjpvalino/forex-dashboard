⚡ STRATEGY RULES
ENTRY LONG Daily ADX ≥ 25 (Trending Up) and Weekly ADX ≥ 20 (Trending Up) — confirmed on 2 consecutive daily bars
ENTRY SHORT Daily ADX ≥ 25 (Trending Down) and Weekly ADX ≥ 20 (Trending Down) — confirmed on 2 consecutive daily bars
STOP LOSS 1.5 × ATR(14) from entry price
TAKE PROFIT 2.5 × ATR(14) from entry price  R:R ≈ 1.67
TRAILING STOP Move stop to breakeven once +1 × ATR profit is reached
MAX HOLD 30 trading bars — exit at market close if SL / TP not hit
RISK / TRADE 1% of current equity per trade  |  Starting equity: $10,000
DATA ~500 daily bars per major pair (~2 years)  |  No look-ahead bias
